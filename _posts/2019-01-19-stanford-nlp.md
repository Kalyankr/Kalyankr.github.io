Are you the kind of person who wants to interact with machines the way you interact
with humans — machine translation, question answering, and more? Then give Natural
Language Processing a try with the open-source Stanford NLP with Deep Learning course.
Stanford delivers high-quality education to the public for free through its YouTube
channel.

Check out this [video](https://youtu.be/8CWyBNX6eDo) to get started with NLP.

To follow along with the course schedule and syllabus, visit the
[CS224n course page](http://web.stanford.edu/class/cs224n/). For the latest news on
Stanford's professional programs in Artificial Intelligence, visit
[Stanford AI](http://learn.stanford.edu/AI.html).
