Resampling helps you use the available data and make models more generalized in a
statistical way. You can learn how effectively the fitted models work. Resampling
is done to improve the accuracy of a model. There are different ways of resampling
data like bootstrapping, cross-validation, repeated cross-validation, etc.

Re-sampling is a series of methods used to reconstruct your sample data sets,
including training sets and validation sets.
